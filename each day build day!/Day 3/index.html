Chocolate Idlis
Quick Recipe

No flour, no cocoa, no syrup, only OREOs 🍪

Ingredients

                - Oreo biscuits chocolate flavour - 2 packs
                - Milk 100ml approx
                - Baking powder - 1/2tbsp
                - Dairy milk or similar chocolate
                - Optional :
                - Sweet/salt Biscuits
                - M&Ms or gems
                - Some dry nuts that you like
                - chocochips

Preparation

Prepare batter :Crush the biscuits and add them into a mixing jar, add about 50ml milk and start the mixer for 3-5 mins. Stop and check the consistency, if too thick then add some more milk.

Mix batter : Now, transfer the batter into a big bowl and add half tbsp baking powder to it, you can also add plain eno if you cannot find baking powder. Add some milk(10-20ml) and whisk the batter properly in order to mix the baking powder.

Greasing : While you’re making the batter, fill the idli container with water and preheat it on medium flame for 10 mins. Now, take the idli stand and grease it with oil a little to prevent the batter from sticking and then start filling it with the batter partially.

Melting chocolate :quickly take a glass lid and keep it on top of a container filled with water and boil it. Keep the dairy milk on top the lid and let it melt.

Baking : Now, add some melted chocolate in between the already filled batter and top it with some more batter. Shake the stand a bit and keep it in the container for baking for 20 mins.

That’s it you’re delicious cakes are ready.. You can also add M&Ms, dry fruits in the batter if you like.

Tip : If you’re not sure about consistency, start with less milk, and keep adding up slowly.
